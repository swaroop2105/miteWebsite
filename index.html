BRIEF ON MITE

Mangalore Institute of Technology & Engineering (MITE) is established by Mr. Rajesh chowta under Rajalaxmi Education Trust, Mangalore in 2007 with an objective of promoting research oriented advance courses in the field of technology & engineering. MITE is committed to promote and propagate quality education on par with International standards to groom talents and turn them into top quality professionals with high intellectual capabilities and strong moral values..
